Contact Tracing App
During COVID, we we're allowed to eat lunch indoors because of the risk of viral spread. The administration wanted us to design a program to monitor where student ate lunch so that in the case of a positive case, they could contact trace. The program uses QR codes posted on lunch rooms to record where students ate each day, and stores the information in a database. There is also an portal for administrators to easily access the information. The linked site is a mock up of the original site, but doesn't link to the database.
