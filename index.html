Contact Tracing App
During COVID, we weren't allowed to eat lunch indoors because of the risk of viral spread. At the request of the administraion, I designed and built a contact-tracing app interface and database that was actually implemented school-wide during 2021. The program used QR codes posted on lunch rooms to record where students ate each day, and stored the information in a database. After students filled out the form, it thanked them with a randomly generated affirmation. There is also a portal for administrators to easily access the information. The linked site is a mock up of the original site, but doesn't link to the database.

Augur Bit Newsletter Design
As our school newspaper transitioned to the web we needed an effective way to reach readers. So I developed and implemented a newsletter template that we send out weekly. It distributes the articles written over the course of the week to the entire student body and faculty as well as our 3,000 web subscribers.
